# Community Sources

This project builds on work from the D&D community. Thanks to everyone who has contributed to making the 5.2.1 SRD more accessible.

## SRD Extractions & Conversions

- **EN World Editable 5.2.1 SRD Thread**
  https://www.enworld.org/threads/editable-5-2-1-srd.716978/
  Community discussion and shared conversions of the SRD to editable formats.

- **springbov/dndsrd5.2_markdown**
  https://github.com/springbov/dndsrd5.2_markdown
  Clean markdown extraction of the 5.2.1 SRD. Used for initial spell and monster imports.

- **dmcb's SRD 5.2.1 Gist**
  https://gist.github.com/dmcb/4b67869f962e3adaa3d0f7e5ca8f4912
  Another community markdown conversion.
