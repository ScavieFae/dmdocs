# Sphinxes

Sphinxes are celestial creatures that guard sacred sites and test those who seek passage with riddles and trials.

## Sphinx Types

|--------|-----|----|----|
| [Sphinx of Lore](lore) | 11 | 17 | 170 |

| [Sphinx of Wonder](wonder) | 1 | 13 | 24 |

## Nature of Sphinxes

Each sphinx embodies a different aspect of divine guardianship. They are patient, immortal beings who take their duties seriously.
