# White Dragon

White dragons are the most bestial and least intelligent of the chromatic dragons. They hunt in arctic regions with savage ferocity.

## Life Stages

| Age | AC | HP | CR |
|-----|----|----|-----|
| [Wyrmling](wyrmling) | 16 | 32 | 2 |
| [Young](young) | 17 | 123 | 6 |
| [Adult](adult) | 18 | 200 | 13 |
| [Ancient](ancient) | 20 | 333 | 20 |

## Ecology

White dragons are chromatic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
