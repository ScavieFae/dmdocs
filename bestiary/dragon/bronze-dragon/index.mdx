# Bronze Dragon

Bronze dragons are coastal dwellers who love to watch ships and sometimes take humanoid form to interact with sailors.

## Life Stages

## Ecology

Bronze dragons are metallic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
