# Red Dragon

Red dragons are the most covetous and arrogant of all chromatic dragons. They dwell in mountainous lairs filled with treasure.

## Life Stages

| Age | AC | HP | CR |
|-----|----|----|-----|
| [Wyrmling](wyrmling) | 17 | 75 | 4 |
| [Young](young) | 18 | 178 | 10 |
| [Adult](adult) | 19 | 256 | 17 |
| [Ancient](ancient) | 22 | 507 | 24 |

## Ecology

Red dragons are chromatic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
