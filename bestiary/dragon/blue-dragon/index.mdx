# Blue Dragon

Blue dragons are vain, territorial creatures that claim vast stretches of desert as their domain. They are master manipulators who prefer to talk before fighting.

## Ecology

Blue dragons are chromatic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
