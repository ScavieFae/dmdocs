# Gold Dragon

Gold dragons are the wisest and most powerful of the metallic dragons. They dedicate themselves to fighting evil.

## Ecology

Gold dragons are metallic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
