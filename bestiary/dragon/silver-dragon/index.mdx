# Silver Dragon

Silver dragons are the most social of metallic dragons, often taking humanoid form to live among people they protect.

## Ecology

Silver dragons are metallic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
