# Green Dragon

Green dragons are manipulative schemers who dwell in ancient forests. They delight in corrupting and controlling other creatures.

## Ecology

Green dragons are chromatic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
