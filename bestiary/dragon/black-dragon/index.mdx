# Black Dragon

Black dragons are cruel, cunning predators that lurk in swamps and marshes. Their acid breath and ambush tactics make them feared throughout the land.

## Ecology

Black dragons are chromatic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
