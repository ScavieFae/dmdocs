# Brass Dragon

Brass dragons are talkative, friendly creatures who dwell in desert regions. They love conversation and collecting stories.

## Ecology

Brass dragons are metallic dragons, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. Like all true dragons, they grow more powerful with age, progressing through four distinct life stages.
