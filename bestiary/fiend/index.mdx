# Fiends

Fiends are evil outsiders from the Lower Planes. The two most prominent types are devils from the Nine Hells and demons from the Abyss, but many other fiendish creatures exist.

## Devils and Demons

| Type | Origin | Alignment | Nature |
|------|--------|-----------|--------|
| [Devils](devils) | Nine Hells | Lawful Evil | Calculating, hierarchical, deal-makers |
| [Demons](demons) | The Abyss | Chaotic Evil | Destructive, chaotic, purely violent |
