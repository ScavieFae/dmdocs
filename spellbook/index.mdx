# Spellbook

Browse spells organized by their school of magic. Each school represents a different approach to manipulating magical energy.

## Schools of Magic

| School | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **[Abjuration](/spellbook/abjuration)** | Protective wards, banishment, and defensive magic |
| **[Conjuration](/spellbook/conjuration)** | Summoning creatures, creating objects, teleportation |
| **[Divination](/spellbook/divination)** | Knowledge, foresight, scrying, and detection |
| **[Enchantment](/spellbook/enchantment)** | Mind-affecting magic, charms, and compulsions |
| **[Evocation](/spellbook/evocation)** | Raw energy manipulation—fire, lightning, force |
| **[Illusion](/spellbook/illusion)** | Deception, phantasms, and sensory manipulation |
| **[Necromancy](/spellbook/necromancy)** | Life force manipulation, death, and undeath |
| **[Transmutation](/spellbook/transmutation)** | Transformation, alteration, and enhancement |

## Spell Components

- **V (Verbal)** — Spoken incantations
- **S (Somatic)** — Precise hand gestures
- **M (Material)** — Physical components (consumed if a cost is listed)

## Special Properties

- **Concentration** — Requires maintained focus; broken by damage or casting another concentration spell
- **Ritual** — Can be cast without expending a spell slot by adding 10 minutes to the casting time
