DMDocs builds on work from the D&D community. Thanks to everyone who has contributed to making the 5.2.1 SRD more accessible.

## Sources

### SRD Extractions & Conversions

- **[EN World Editable 5.2.1 SRD Thread](https://www.enworld.org/threads/editable-5-2-1-srd.716978/)** — Community discussion and shared conversions of the SRD to editable formats.

- **[springbov/dndsrd5.2_markdown](https://github.com/springbov/dndsrd5.2_markdown)** — Clean markdown extraction of the 5.2.1 SRD. Used for initial spell and monster imports.

- **[dmcb's SRD 5.2.1 Gist](https://gist.github.com/dmcb/4b67869f962e3adaa3d0f7e5ca8f4912)** — Another community markdown conversion.

### Tools & References

- **[mshea/lazy_gm_tools](https://github.com/mshea/lazy_gm_tools)** — Sly Flourish's Lazy GM tools and resources.

### Official Source

- **[System Reference Document 5.2.1](https://www.dndbeyond.com/srd)** — The official SRD from Wizards of the Coast, licensed under CC-BY-4.0.
