Choose your class to define your character's capabilities, from martial prowess to magical power.

## The Twelve Classes

| Class | Hit Die | Primary Ability | Saving Throws | Armor & Weapons |
|-------|---------|-----------------|---------------|-----------------|
| [Barbarian](/docs/classes/barbarian) | d12 | Strength | Str, Con | Light/Medium armor, shields, simple/martial weapons |
| [Bard](/docs/classes/bard) | d8 | Charisma | Dex, Cha | Light armor, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords |
| [Cleric](/docs/classes/cleric) | d8 | Wisdom | Wis, Cha | Light/Medium armor, shields, simple weapons |
| [Druid](/docs/classes/druid) | d8 | Wisdom | Int, Wis | Light/Medium armor (nonmetal), shields (nonmetal), simple weapons |
| [Fighter](/docs/classes/fighter) | d10 | Str or Dex | Str, Con | All armor, shields, simple/martial weapons |
| [Monk](/docs/classes/monk) | d8 | Dex & Wis | Str, Dex | Simple weapons, shortswords |
| [Paladin](/docs/classes/paladin) | d10 | Str & Cha | Wis, Cha | All armor, shields, simple/martial weapons |
| [Ranger](/docs/classes/ranger) | d10 | Dex & Wis | Str, Dex | Light/Medium armor, shields, simple/martial weapons |
| [Rogue](/docs/classes/rogue) | d8 | Dexterity | Dex, Int | Light armor, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords |
| [Sorcerer](/docs/classes/sorcerer) | d6 | Charisma | Con, Cha | Simple weapons |
| [Warlock](/docs/classes/warlock) | d8 | Charisma | Wis, Cha | Light armor, simple weapons |
| [Wizard](/docs/classes/wizard) | d6 | Intelligence | Int, Wis | Simple weapons |

## Spellcasting Classes

Classes with access to spells: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

## Subclasses

Each class has one subclass included in the SRD:

- Barbarian: Path of the Berserker
- Bard: College of Lore
- Cleric: Life Domain
- Druid: Circle of the Land
- Fighter: Champion
- Monk: Warrior of the Open Hand
- Paladin: Oath of Devotion
- Ranger: Hunter
- Rogue: Thief
- Sorcerer: Draconic Sorcery
- Warlock: Fiend Patron
- Wizard: Evoker
