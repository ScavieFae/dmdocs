---
title: Hirelings
description: Skilled and untrained hirelings for hire
---

Skilled hirelings include anyone hired to perform a service that involves a proficiency (including weapon, tool, or skill): a mercenary, an artisan, a scribe, or the like. The pay shown is a minimum; some expert hirelings require more pay.

Untrained hirelings are hired for work that requires no particular proficiencies; they include laborers and porters.

## Hireling Costs

| Service | Cost |
|---------|------|
| Skilled hireling | 2 GP per day |
| Untrained hireling | 2 SP per day |
| Messenger | 2 CP per mile |
