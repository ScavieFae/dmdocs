## Spell Schools

| School | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Abjuration | Protective and warding magic |
| Conjuration | Summoning creatures and teleportation |
| Divination | Knowledge, foresight, and detection |
| Enchantment | Mind-affecting and charm magic |
| Evocation | Energy manipulation and damage |
| Illusion | Deception and sensory manipulation |
| Necromancy | Life force, death, and undeath |
| Transmutation | Transformation and alteration |

## Spell Components

- **V (Verbal)** — Spoken words
- **S (Somatic)** — Hand gestures
- **M (Material)** — Physical components (consumed if cost listed)
- **C (Concentration)** — Requires maintained focus
- **R (Ritual)** — Can be cast as a ritual (adds 10 minutes)
