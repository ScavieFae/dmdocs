Wondrous treasures imbued with magical power.

## Rarity

| Rarity | Typical Value | Character Level |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| Common | 50-100 GP | 1st+ |
| Uncommon | 101-500 GP | 1st+ |
| Rare | 501-5,000 GP | 5th+ |
| Very Rare | 5,001-50,000 GP | 11th+ |
| Legendary | 50,001+ GP | 17th+ |

## Categories

- **Armor** — Magical protection
- **Potions** — Consumable magical liquids
- **Rings** — Magical finger bands
- **Rods** — Scepter-like magic items
- **Scrolls** — Single-use spell containers
- **Staffs** — Spellcasting implements
- **Wands** — Slender spellcasting tools
- **Weapons** — Magical arms
- **Wondrous Items** — Everything else

## Attunement

Some items require attunement—a special bond formed during a Short Rest. A creature can be attuned to no more than 3 magic items at a time.
