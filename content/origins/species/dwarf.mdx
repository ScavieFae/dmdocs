Dwarves are a storied people, known for their resilience and craftsmanship. Created by the god Moradin, dwarves originally carved great cities into the mountains. Though many dwarves still dwell in their ancestral mountain homes, others have ventured out to join the wider world, bringing their determination with them.

## Special Traits

### Darkvision

You have Darkvision with a range of 120 feet.

### Dwarven Resilience

You have Resistance to Poison damage. You also have Advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the Poisoned condition.

### Dwarven Toughness

Your Hit Point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 again whenever you gain a level.

### Stonecunning

As a Bonus Action, you gain Tremorsense with a range of 60 feet for 10 minutes. You must be on a stone surface or touching such a surface to use this Tremorsense. The stone can be natural or worked.

You can use this Bonus Action a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
