Your character's origin combines a **background** (their history) with a **species** (their ancestry).

## Backgrounds

Your background describes what your character did before becoming an adventurer. See [Backgrounds](/origins/backgrounds) for the four available backgrounds: Acolyte, Criminal, Sage, and Soldier.

## Species

Your species determines your physical characteristics and innate abilities.

- [Dragonborn](/origins/species/dragonborn) — Draconic heritage
- [Dwarf](/origins/species/dwarf) — Stout and hardy
- [Elf](/origins/species/elf) — Graceful and long-lived
- [Gnome](/origins/species/gnome) — Small and clever
- [Goliath](/origins/species/goliath) — Towering strength
- [Halfling](/origins/species/halfling) — Small and lucky
- [Human](/origins/species/human) — Versatile and ambitious
- [Orc](/origins/species/orc) — Powerful and enduring
- [Tiefling](/origins/species/tiefling) — Infernal heritage
