Your character's origin combines a **background** (their history) with a **species** (their ancestry).

## Backgrounds

Your background describes what your character did before becoming an adventurer.

- [Acolyte](/docs/origins/backgrounds/acolyte) — Religious service
- [Criminal](/docs/origins/backgrounds/criminal) — Life outside the law
- [Sage](/docs/origins/backgrounds/sage) — Scholarly pursuits
- [Soldier](/docs/origins/backgrounds/soldier) — Military service

## Species

Your species determines your physical characteristics and innate abilities.

- [Dragonborn](/docs/origins/species/dragonborn) — Draconic heritage
- [Dwarf](/docs/origins/species/dwarf) — Stout and hardy
- [Elf](/docs/origins/species/elf) — Graceful and long-lived
- [Gnome](/docs/origins/species/gnome) — Small and clever
- [Goliath](/docs/origins/species/goliath) — Towering strength
- [Halfling](/docs/origins/species/halfling) — Small and lucky
- [Human](/docs/origins/species/human) — Versatile and ambitious
- [Orc](/docs/origins/species/orc) — Powerful and enduring
- [Tiefling](/docs/origins/species/tiefling) — Infernal heritage
