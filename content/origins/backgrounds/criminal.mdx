title: Criminal
description: A character with a history of breaking the law and evading authority

You eked out a living in dark alleyways, cutting purses or burgling shops. Perhaps you were part of a thieves' guild or simply worked alone. Whatever your methods, you've developed a knack for unsavory work and have connections to the criminal underworld.

| Trait | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ability Scores | +2 Dexterity, +1 Intelligence, or +1 Dexterity and +1 Intelligence and +1 to one other |
| Skill Proficiencies | Sleight of Hand, Stealth |
| Tool Proficiency | Thieves' Tools |
| Equipment | Crowbar, Dagger (2), Thieves' Tools, Traveler's Clothes, 16 GP |
| Origin Feat | Alert |

## Alert

*Origin Feat*

Always on the lookout for danger, you gain the following benefits:

**Initiative Proficiency.** When you roll Initiative, you can add your Proficiency Bonus to the roll.

**Initiative Swap.** Immediately after you roll Initiative, you can swap your Initiative with the Initiative of one willing ally in the same combat. You can't make this swap if you or the ally has the Incapacitated condition.
