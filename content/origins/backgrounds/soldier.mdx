---
title: Soldier
description: A character trained in the arts of war and military discipline
---

You trained in youth to become a soldier. You learned the basics of combat, how to read tactical maps, and how to operate within the structure of a military unit. You might have been part of a standing army, a mercenary company, or a militia.

| Trait | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ability Scores | +2 Strength, +1 Constitution, or +1 Strength and +1 Constitution and +1 to one other |
| Skill Proficiencies | Athletics, Intimidation |
| Tool Proficiency | Gaming Set (one of your choice) |
| Equipment | Gaming Set (same as above), Healer's Kit, Quiver, Shortbow, 20 Arrows, Spear, Traveler's Clothes, 14 GP |
| Origin Feat | Savage Attacker |

## Savage Attacker

*Origin Feat*

You've trained to deal particularly damaging strikes. Once per turn when you hit a target with a weapon, you can roll the weapon's damage dice twice and use either roll against the target.
